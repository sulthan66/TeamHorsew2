Welcome to the Guiding Light College Alumni Association

The Guiding Light College Alumni Association maintains and enhances a highly engaged, vibrant community of alumni and friends worldwide.
It helps connect alumni to the University and each other through a wealth of community activities, continuing education programs, online services, and events around the world.

The Guiding Light College Alumni Association (GLCA) is the official association for you and the more than 400,000 Guiding Light College alumni around the world. You have a membership just by attending Guiding Light College or one of Guiding Light Graduate and Professional Schools.

Here at the HAA, we connect you and your fellow graduates with a wealth of networking opportunities, professional development resources, programs, volunteer opportunities, faculty-led trips, and more.
